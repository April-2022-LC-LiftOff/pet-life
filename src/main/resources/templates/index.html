About

PetLife is your pet/animal virtual organizer, which allows pet owners and veterinarians to share information with each other. Pet owner users can sign up for an account and log in to create profiles of their pets. With the account, they can insert and update their pets' information covering from biographical to medical, like name, sex, behaviors, species, allergies, etc. Alternatively, veterinarian users can update and insert medical information for a specific pet, and then the owner would know what is happening to their pets medically.


Key Features

  • Easily organize all information of your animals in a single place, covering from biographical to medical.
  • As an owner, to create, read, update and delete pet profiles are quick and easy.
  • Auto update pet age and calculate age when inputting birthday.
  • Convert weight units between pound and kilogram.
  • As a veterinarian, you can read and update all pets' medical information.
  • Simply share the information between owners and veterinarians.
